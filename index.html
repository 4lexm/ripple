ripple

Fubu-inspired dependency management

ripple was originally created to aide the Fubu team in managing large numbers of dependencies both locally and in continuation integration environments. It was designed to fill a gap in usage found with the NuGet client.

Why not use NuGet?

NuGet worked great for "Fixed" versions of dependencies. However, our differing opinions quickly began to cause friction. We believe that all of our projects should be built against the latest of their Fubu dependencies. For example, any of our projects that depend on FubuMVC.Core.dll should *always* be built against the *latest* version of FubuMVC.Core.dll. We still utilize NuGet feeds but we just layer on additional concepts - namely Float vs. Fixed dependencies and feeds.
